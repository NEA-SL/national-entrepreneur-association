About NEA: Cultivating Sri Lanka's Entrepreneurial Renaissance

In the vibrant tapestry of Sri Lanka, a wealth of entrepreneurial spirit thrives, yet often, this potential remains untapped, hampered by systemic voids. NEA (National Entrepreneurship Alliance) emerged from this very realization—a resolute commitment to dismantle the isolation, underfunding, and lack of recognition that often beset the nation's most innovative minds. We are not merely an organization; we are a **movement** poised to redefine the landscape of Sri Lankan enterprise.

Our Vision: A Unified Force for Progress

NEA aspires to be the seminal force in Sri Lanka's entrepreneurial journey, a beacon uniting visionary founders, discerning investors, and transformative changemakers. Our ambition is clear: to forge a vibrant ecosystem where ideas are seamlessly transmuted into capital, talent finds its true north through mentorship, and passion ignites into decisive action.

  • Empowering Growth: Providing bespoke startup guidance, unparalleled funding connections, and strategic blueprints for scalable expansion.
  • Shaping Policy: Advocating for and influencing national-level decisions to cultivate an environment unequivocally conducive to entrepreneurial success.
  • Fostering Inclusivity: Extending our reach to uplift and support underserved and rural sectors, ensuring no talent is left behind.
  • Pioneering a Model: Crafting a replicable blueprint for entrepreneurship that serves as an exemplar for developing economies worldwide.

The Genesis: A Founder's Unwavering Conviction

At the heart of NEA's inception lies the unwavering conviction of its founder, **Mohommed Thalaal Marikar**. His journey is a testament to the transformative power of grassroots engagement—forged through years of gritty entrepreneurial hustle, from nurturing nascent restaurants to orchestrating impactful community-centric initiatives. Thalaal embodies the ethos of practical, ground-up change, deeply committed to empowering Sri Lanka's vibrant youth to realize their full potential. NEA is the embodiment of his vision: a future where every aspiring entrepreneur finds the fertile ground to flourish.

Strategic Consultancy

We provide free or affordable advisory sessions for startups and SMEs, offering tailored insights and practical strategies to navigate the entrepreneurial landscape.

Investment Opportunities

We connect scalable businesses with a network of local and international investors, helping promising ventures secure the capital they need to grow and thrive.

National Summits & Policy Advocacy

We host impactful discussions between decision-makers and entrepreneurs, and actively work to shape entrepreneurship-friendly policies at a national level.

Education & Mentorship

Through workshops, digital content, and personalized guidance, we equip entrepreneurs with the knowledge and skills necessary for success.

Regional Chapters & Community Building

We expand our support to rural and underserved regions, building partnerships and fostering a national directory of verified entrepreneurs.

Our Approach: Building a Robust Ecosystem

  • Strategic Partnerships

    We forge strong alliances with companies eager to hire, mentor, or invest in promising entrepreneurs, creating a symbiotic network of support.

  • Inspiring Storytelling

    Through compelling narratives, we illuminate the journeys of real founders, showcasing their triumphs and challenges to inspire the next generation.

  • Verified Entrepreneur Directory

    We are building a comprehensive national directory of verified entrepreneurs, fostering credibility and facilitating connections within the community.

  • Community Engagement

    Leveraging technology and proactive outreach, we ensure our community remains informed, connected, and actively engaged in the entrepreneurial dialogue.
